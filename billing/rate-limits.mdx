## What are rate limits?
Rate limits control how many requests you can make per second. When rate limits are exceeded, you'll receive an HTTP 429 response.

### Increase Rate Limits
Teams on Professional plans can purchase an extra 100 RPS for $100/month. If you need custom rate limits ahead of launches, [contact our sales team](https://form.typeform.com/to/KiacmxpZ). 

If you are on Developer or Business tier, please upgrade your plan to increase your rate limits.
