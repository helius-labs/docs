## Our RPC Node Solutions

### Shared RPC Plans

Our shared RPC plans provide cost-effective access to Solana's blockchain with tiered rate limits. Perfect for:
- Development and testing
- Small to medium applications
- Projects with predictable traffic patterns

### Dedicated Nodes

[Private Dedicated Nodes](/solana-rpc-nodes/dedicated-nodes) offer maximum performance with no rate limits, making them ideal for:
- High-volume applications
- Latency-sensitive operations like trading
- Projects requiring consistent performance

### Enterprise Solutions

For organizations with unique requirements, we offer customized infrastructure solutions including:
- Multi-region node clusters
- Custom Geyser plugins
- Specialized node configurations
- Dedicated support teams
