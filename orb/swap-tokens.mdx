Block explorers like [Orb](https://orb.helius.dev) are great tools for [finding top Solana coins](https://www.helius.dev/orb/explore-markets), researching projects, and deciding whether to buy or sell tokens. 

To make this process even easier, we’ve added a “Buy” button to every Solana token page indexed on the Orb explorer. All you need to do is connect your wallet and click “Buy” to swap into or out of any SPL token on Solana.

This tutorial explains how to swap tokens on Solana using Orb, along with keyboard shortcuts for faster research and easier buys.

## **Swapping Solana Tokens on Orb**

Here is a step-by-step tutorial on finding and trading tokens on Orb.

### **Find the Token**

First, find the token you’re looking to swap by either searching by name in Orb’s search bar or by entering the token’s mint address.

<Frame caption="Orb token search bar showing the ZEC token on Solana">
  ![Find Zec Token On Solana Using The Orb Explorer Jp](/images/find-zec-token-on-solana-using-the-orb-explorer.jpg)
</Frame>

For more details, read these tutorials on [looking up Solana tokens](https://www.helius.dev/docs/orb/explore-tokens) and [finding SPL mint addresses](https://www.helius.dev/docs/orb/explore-authorities) .

### Click “Buy”

At the top of the page, next to the token’s image and name, click the orange “Buy” button.

You will also see key token metrics, including supply, market cap, liquidity, fully diluted value (FDV), current price, holders (on Solana), and 24-hour volume.

<Frame caption="Solana token page on the Orb explorer with the “Buy” button highlighted">
  ![Zec Token Explorer And Swap Button On Orb Jp](/images/zec-token-explorer-and-swap-button-on-orb.jpg)
</Frame>

For this example, we’ll swap SOL for ZEC (Zcash) on Solana.

### Connect Your Wallet

Clicking the “Buy” button or pressing the keyboard shortcut “B” opens a dialogue box prompting you to connect your wallet.

<Frame caption="Dialogue box to connect your wallet, powered by reown">
  ![Connect Solana Wallet To The Orb Explorer To Swap Tokens Jp](/images/connect-solana-wallet-to-the-orb-explorer-to-swap-tokens.jpg)
</Frame>

### Use the Swap Widget

Once connected, you’ll see the main swap widget.

<Frame caption="Orb swap widget indicating a 0.015 SOL swap for ZEC">
  ![The Orb Explorer Swap Widget For Trading Tokens On Solana Jp](/images/the-orb-explorer-swap-widget-for-trading-tokens-on-solana.jpg)
</Frame>

This swap widget is a simple UI that lets you choose the asset and amount you want to buy/sell. 

Once you click “Buy ZEC,” your wallet will display a confirmation message.

<Frame caption="Phantom swap confirmation message for trading on Orb">
  ![Phantom Wallet Swap Confirmation For Trading On The Orb Explorer Jp](/images/phantom-wallet-swap-confirmation-for-trading-on-the-orb-explorer.jpg)
</Frame>

Once you click confirm, your trade will be routed through [DFlow’s](https://dflow.net/) meta aggregator or Jupiter. DFlow’s intelligent transaction execution stack offers traders the best prices and tightest spreads.

<Info>
  A 0.1% swap fee is applied to all swaps made through the Orb UI.
</Info>

### View Swap Details

Once the swap is completed, you’ll see a swap confirmation message and a link to [view the transaction details](https://www.helius.dev/docs/orb/explore-transactions). Click this link to see a summary of your trade.

<Frame caption="Swap transaction summary page on the Orb explorer">
  ![Sol Zec Swap Transaction Explorer Summary On The Orb Jp](/images/sol-zec-swap-transaction-explorer-summary-on-the-orb.jpg)
</Frame>

That’s it! 

You’ve successfully swapped Solana for ZEC on Orb.

To [view your wallet balances](https://www.helius.dev/docs/orb/explore-wallets), go to your wallet page on Orb and click the **Tokens** tab.

<Frame caption="Explore token balances for any wallet on Orb">
  ![Solana Wallet Explorer Tab On Orb Jp](/images/solana-wallet-explorer-tab-on-orb.jpg)
</Frame>
