## Using WebSockets

<Accordion title="How much faster are Enhanced WebSockets vs. Standard WebSockets?">
Enhanced Websockets are, on average, 1.5x - 2x faster than standard websockets.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why are Enhanced WebSockets disconnecting?">
Implement reconnection and ping logic as shown in our [documentation](https://docs.helius.dev/data-streaming/enhanced-websockets-beta#transaction-subscribe-example), then add your functionalities on top.
</Accordion>
