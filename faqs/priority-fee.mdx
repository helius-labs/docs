## Transaction Issues

<Accordion title="Why aren't my sendTransaction requests landing?">
Call the **Priority Fee API**, optimize compute‑unit usage, and send the transaction with `skipPreflight: true`. See our [**Transaction Optimization Guide**](https://www.helius.dev/docs/sending-transactions/optimizing-transactions) for a step‑by‑step walkthrough.
</Accordion>

## Error Handling

<Accordion title="Why am I suddenly seeing 5xx errors?">
Check our [status page](https://helius.statuspage.io/) for relevant updates. If there's no related incident, contact support.
</Accordion>
