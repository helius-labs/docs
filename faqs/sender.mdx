## About Sender

<Accordion title="What is Sender and why is it faster?">
Sender broadcasts transactions to Jito and SW-QoS validators in parallel. With seven regional endpoints, typical land-time is ≤ 1.5 slots.
</Accordion>

## Setting Tips & Fees

<Accordion title="How do I set tips & priority fees?">
See the Sender documentation for [examples](https://www.helius.dev/docs/sending-transactions/sender#simple-code-example).
</Accordion>

## Rate Limits

<Accordion title="Are there rate limits?">
Yes—standard limits apply. Currently 6 TPS per region.
</Accordion>
