## RPC Methods

<Accordion title="How do I authenticate RPC requests?">
Add your Helius API key as the `?api-key=` query parameter.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Our alerts show sendTransaction land-rate below 90%. What do you recommend?">
Call the Priority Fee API, optimize compute-unit usage, and send with `skipPreflight: true`. See the [Transaction Optimization Guide](https://www.helius.dev/docs/sending-transactions/optimizing-transactions) for full details.
</Accordion>
