## Standard Plans

Access a shared RPC node fleet across New York, Ashburn, Chicago, Vancouver, Los Angeles, Singapore, Tokyo, Frankfurt, and the UK, automatically routing requests to the nearest node.

### Features

- RPC calls
- Webhooks
- Standard WebSockets
- Enhanced WebSockets (beta) — available for Business and Professional plans only
- DAS APIs
- Enhanced Transactions API
- Priority Fee API
- Token Balance API
- Photon Methods (ZK Compression)
- [Shared Staked Connections](/sending-transactions/staked-connections.mdx) for `sendTransaction`;

Each plan has a set amount of credits and rate limits for different calls.

## Credits

A clear breakdown of the credit costs in our Standard plans.

- **Standard RPC Calls**: 1 credit per call
  - **Exceptions**:
    - [`getProgramAccounts`](/rpc/http/getprogramaccounts) : 10 credits
    - **Archival Calls:** 10 credits each
      - [`getTransaction`](/rpc/http/gettransaction)
      - [`getBlock`](/rpc/http/getblock)
      - [`getBlocks`](/rpc/http/getblocks)
      - [`getInflationReward`](/rpc/http/getinflationreward)
      - [`getSignaturesForAddress`](/rpc/http/getsignaturesforaddress)
      - [`getBlockTime`](/rpc/http/getblocktime)
    - **Send Transaction**:;
      - Via [https://mainnet.helius-rpc.com](https://mainnet.helius-rpc.com) : 1 credit
      - Via [https://staked.helius-rpc.com](https://staked.helius-rpc.com) : 50 credits
    - **Priority Fee API**: 1 credit
- **DAS Calls**: 10 credits each
  - [`getAsset`](/das-api/getasset)
  - [`getAssetProof`](/das-api/getassetproof)
  - [`getAssetsByOwner`](/das-api/getassetsbyowner)
  - [`getAssetsByAuthority`](/das-api/getassetsbyauthority)
  - [`getAssetsByCreator`](/das-api/getassetsbycreator)
  - [`getAssetsByGroup`](/das-api/getassetsbygroup)
  - [`searchAssets`](/das-api/searchassets)
  - [`getSignaturesForAsset`](/das-api/getsignaturesforasset)
  - [`getTokenAccounts`](/das-api/gettokenaccounts)
  - [`getNFTEditions`](/das-api/getnfteditions)
- **Photon Calls:**
  - Standard Calls: 10 credits
  - ;[`getValidityProofs`](/zk-compression/getvalidityproof): 100 credits (due to higher computation)
- **Webhook**:
  - Webhook Pushes: 1 credit
  - Webhook Updates via [API](http://localhost:3000/webhooks/update-webhook): 100 credits
- **Enhanced Transactions API and Mint API**: 100 credits per call

### Extra Credits

If you exhaust your credits within a billing cycle, you can purchase additional credits:

- **Crypto Plans —** purchase additional prepaid credits directly from the dashboard;
- **Card Payment Plans —** enable autoscaling in the Project Settings on the dashboard

We've expanded our documentation on prepaid credits and autoscaling. For detailed information and step-by-step guidance, please visit our updated [Credits and Autoscaling Guide](/prepaid-credits-and-autoscaling).

## Enterprise Plans

Enterprise plans are ideal for large teams that need personalized rate limits and more credits than the professional plan offers. Enterprise customers receive discounted pricing for high credit volumes and longer commitments. You can choose your support level with service level agreements (SLAs), uptime guarantees, usage alerts, and direct access to our engineering team to help you optimize your setup.
