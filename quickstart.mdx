---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for a Free Helius Account">
    Head over to the [Helius Dashboard](https://dev.helius.xyz/dashboard) and create your free account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grab Your API Key">
    Go to the [API Keys](https://dashboard.helius.dev/api-keys) section of your dashboard and copy your key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make Your First API Call">
    Replace `YOUR_API_KEY` with your key, then run this command in your terminal:

    ```bash
    curl --request POST \
      --url 'https://mainnet.helius-rpc.com/?api-key=YOUR_API_KEY' \
      --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
      --data '{
      "jsonrpc": "2.0",
      "id": "1",
      "method": "getAssetsByOwner",
      "params": {
        "ownerAddress": "86xCnPeV69n6t3DnyGvkKobf9FdN2H9oiVDdaMpo2MMY"
      }  
    }'
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Success!" icon="check">
    Success! You’ve fetched all assets owned by the address `86xCn…o2MMY` which is the wallet address of [Anatoly Yakovenko](https://x.com/aeyakovenko), one of the founders of Solana. Now try it with one of your own wallet addresses.
  </Step>
</Steps>
